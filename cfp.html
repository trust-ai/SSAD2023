Call for Papers

We invite submissions that have at most 6 pages with unlimited bibliography and appendix.

Topics include but are not limited to:

  • Robust perception algorithms against adversarial attack
  • Physical attack and defense to autonomous driving systems
  • Data poisoning and defense to autonomous driving systems
  • Robust decision-making algorithms, e.g., Robust RL
  • Safe decision-making and control algorithms, e.g., Safe RL
  • Trajectory and behavior prediction with uncertainty
  • Causal discovery and counterfactual analysis of traffic scenarios
  • Behavior modeling of pedestrian and vehicle
  • Safety verification and certification
  • Neuro-symbolic approaches in autonomous driving
  • Knowledge representation and reasoning in autonomous driving
  • Autonomous driving datasets, simulation, evaluations, and metrics
Reviewing will be performed in double-blind, with criteria include:
  • Quality of the methodology and experiments
  • Novelty
  • Relevance
  • Societal impacts

Author Instructions

Papers should be submitted through OpenReview.

Submission Format

Submitted papers are recommended to have at most 6 pages with unlimited bibliography and appendix, using CVPR template. Accepted papers will be hosted on this workshop website but will not be archived in the CVPRW proceedings and can be submitted to other workshops, conferences or journals if their submission policy allows.

Dual submission policy

We allow dual submission with other workshops or conferences. You can also submit papers that have some overlaps with your recently published papers. However, we disallow papers that are presented at the CVPR main conference as well as other machine learning conferences.
